Support and partnerships

Support and cooperation with other partners is crucial to the success of our work. In order to devote the necessary time to creating quality research, infographics and explainers, we need sufficient funding. That also helps us communicate our findings and thus contribute to a more constructive debate on climate change and to data-driven decisions towards a net-zero society.

In addition to individual donors, our work has benefited from significant support from several companies and institutions (below, we list institutions that have provided us financial support of over 4,000 EUR). Thank you!
